<br> #### Class 2 - 2D Design and Cutting During my labs on June 22, I designed and constructed a press-fit hexagonal pencil holder. My first few designs were extremely complicated. So, after much consideration, I decided to use something I had previously designed to compensate for my lack of knowledge on a laser cutter. I first drew my design on paper to make sure I had the correct plans. Then, I reconstructed the pencil holder on a website named Shapr3D. From there, I took the plans to the laser cutter. After cutting out my design, the pieces fit together perfectly. However, my height dimensions were slightly off. My pencil holder was shorter than I imagined it would be. While I am still getting used to using the metric system, my ability in understanding it is improving every day.
